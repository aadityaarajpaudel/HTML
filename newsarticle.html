SET Council Hosts 2nd SXC Walkathon

By RSS

Kathmandu, February 2, 2025 – The St. Xavier’s College (SXC) Walkathon, organized annually by the SET Council, took place on Saturday with overwhelming participation and a strong message of social inclusion. This particularly aimed to raise awareness about the challenges faced by differently-abled individuals and to support various charitable initiatives.

The walk commenced and concluded at St. Xavier’s College in Maitighar with a stop at Patan Durbar Square. Participation of differently-abled individuals in a special wheelchair rally, emphasized on the need for greater accessibility and inclusivity in society.

Participants shared that it was incredibly moving to witness so many people coming together for such an important cause and events like this remind us that support from society can have a lasting impact and bring about positive change.

The Walkathon not only aimed to raise funds but also served as a platform for spreading awareness about social justice, equality, and the importance of accessibility. The event brought together students, faculty members, and citizens from the local community, uniting them in the shared goal of creating a more inclusive society.
